Hi! My name is Melanie Malek. My experience in web design began during my first year of university where I developed a dynamic marketing website for the course SEG2900. My design process is thoughtful and user-centered. I begin by understanding the user's needs, then move into prototyping before refining the visual style to ensure accessibility and clarity. I aim for clean, modern interfaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. I'm currently expanding my skills through a user interface design course, where I'm learning more about design principles, usability, and accessibility. This portfolio showcases my journey in learning and applying best practices in UI design.
